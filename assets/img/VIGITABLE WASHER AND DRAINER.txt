Features of a Vegetable Wash and Drainer
2-in-1 or Multi-Functional Design,
Washing Bowl and Strainer/Colander: The most common design is a bowl-within-a-bowl or a bowl paired with a separate strainer. This allows you to soak vegetables in the outer bowl and then lift or rotate the inner strainer to drain the water quickly without pouring the contents into the sink.
Washing, Rinsing, and Serving: Designed to handle all three tasks, minimizing the number of dishes used.
Efficient Drainage
Perforated Sides/Bottom: The strainer component has holes or slots designed for quick and efficient water drainage.
